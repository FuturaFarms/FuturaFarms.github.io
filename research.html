Research
Developing Tomorrow's Cultivars
We have 2 acres dedicated to trials of prairie hardy trees and shrubs in conjunction with Prairie Shade Consulting. Species are evaluated for many specific traits that include (but are not limited to) cold hardiness, disease resistance, columnar crown, and fall colour.

New cultivars of trees and shrubs are continually being evaluated and introduced to aid our urban forest plantings adapt to new pests such as Dutch elm disease and the emerald ash borer, a changing climate, and constantly evolving consumer preferences.
